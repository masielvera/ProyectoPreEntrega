ABOUT

My name is Masiel Vera, I am a nature photographer based in Colombia. My passion for photography began as a hobby a few years ago and grew with each trip I made to connect with nature and her surroundings. When I had my first semi-professional camera I knew that I wanted to gain experience and polish my eye to portray what I was seeing, I took courses and participated in workshops that helped me train and obtain better photographs. From there, my spirit of adventure and passion for photography has grown, which has led me to want to teach and share this passion with people who want to follow this path. ¡Join the adventure!
